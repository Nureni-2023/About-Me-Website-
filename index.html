Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of websites do you build?

I specialize in building custom websites, including responsive landing pages, business websites, portfolios, and small e-commerce sites. My focus is on modern, performant, and user-friendly solutions.

What technologies do you use?

My primary stack includes HTML, CSS (with Tailwind CSS for rapid development), and JavaScript. For more complex applications, I can work with frameworks like React. I also focus on clean, semantic HTML and accessibility.

What is your development process?

My process typically involves initial consultation, planning and wireframing, design mockups, development, testing, deployment, and optional ongoing maintenance. I believe in clear communication throughout the project.
